Welcome to my website. I am currently a final year student in IIT Madras, pursuing integrated Bachelor and Master of Science in Physics. Born and brought up in Kerala, I've been in Chennai since I started my academic career in IITM.
Constantly updating myself with new trends in the field of website development and specializing in them, the dedication I show to assigned work helps me complete it with satisfaction on both sides.

I am a precision artist; I hate those unnecessary variables and newlines, call it my OCD. I prefer learning on the way over remembering prototypes. I'm ready to take on any task in any of the fields mentioned above, unless I'm busy with more than two projects at a time or my semester exams are going on. And I will submit satisfactory result.
