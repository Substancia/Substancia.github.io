Welcome to my website. I am currently a third year student at IIT Madras, pursuing integrated Bachelor and Master of Science in Physics. Born and brought up in Kerala, I've been in Chennai since I started my acaemic career in IITM. I'm still a noob in the field of website development, but the dedication I show to assigned work helps me complete it with satisfaction on both sides. I'm more of a finish-it-today kind of person. I'm ready to take on any task in any of the fields mentioned above, unless I'm busy with more than two projects at a time or my semester exams are going on. And I will submit satisfactory result.
