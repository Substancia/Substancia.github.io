NAGALAPURAM, ANDHRA PRADESH


Nagalapuram is a village in Chittoor district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.
Nagalapuram is located at 13.4000°N 79.7833°E. It has an average elevation of 64 meters (213 feet).
Nagalapuram Falls is a waterfall which attracts many tourists.
