RANIPURAM, KASARGOD, KERALA


Ranipuram is a hill station in the Indian state of Kerala.
Ranipuram is located at 12.4258889°N 75.3416631°E. Situated in the Vellarikundu taluk, Kasargod District, Ranipuram mountain peak rises to 1022 metres above the sea level. It is 45 km away from nearest municipality Kanhangad. It is known as "Ooty of Kerala".
